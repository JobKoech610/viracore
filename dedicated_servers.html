FAQs

Why choose a dedicated server?
With a dedicated server, you have complete control. You don't share resources with others, so you can use it however you want.
What are the benefits of dedicated resources?
Dedicated resources mean no restrictions. You can use all the power and flexibility of the server without worrying about limits.
Is a dedicated server more secure?
Yes, dedicated servers offer more security. Since you're the only one using it, there's less risk of someone else accessing your data or messing with your settings.
